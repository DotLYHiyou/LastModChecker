LastModChecker

Enter a URL, and you will get the time the page was last modified.

It might take a minute or two, a proxy is in the back to get around certain restrictions.

Types of responses:

1. (A success) Day of the week, day number, month, year, time (GMT)

2. (An error) "Error fetching the URL."

3. (Not found) "No Last-Modified header found."
